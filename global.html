angleToVector(angle) → {Vector2}

Transforms a angle in degrees to a position. For example when the angle is 0 the vector will be (1, 0) and for an angle of 90 the vector will be (0, 1) please keep in mind that when used with really large angles (100000 degrees) this function will not be accurate
